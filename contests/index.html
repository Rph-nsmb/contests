Contests

Introducing NSMB Contest #1!

Star Coin hiding contest

Use your creativity to remake a level by hiding its star coins! Will we find them?

Rules

The level has to use the vanilla assets. Custom graphics will not be needed.
Try to make your Star Coins challenging to catch/find, but still rewarding. Making the Star coins super hard to reach (or super hard to find) will not help you.
The level can be modified, but it has to be similar to the original (For example, you can do slight edits or add a sub-area in the style of the vanilla level).
The Star Coins have to be reachable. We may ask you to send us a video to prove it.
Those rules are in addition to our contest rules (you can find them on our Discord).
